As ADA University students studying engineering and information technology, "Ibrahim Aghazada", "Huseyn Huseynli", "Uzeyir Bakhishzada","Avazagha Suleymanzade", and "Vidadi Nebiyev" got together and made the decision to hold the Hour of Code event at one of Baku's secondary schools. Working together, we gave a fifth-grade class a quick rundown of ADA University. To help students understand the logic underlying programming languages, we also provided a brief overview of programming languages and showed them how to use Code.org's drag-and-drop learning tool. In order to motivate the students, we awarded diplomas to all of students in our class.

On November 7, 2024, at 11:00, the 5B class students of Binagadi District Secondary School No. 246 in Baku participated in the Hour of Code event organized by ADA University students Huseyn Huseynli, Ibrahim Aghazada, Uzeyir Bakhishzada, Vidadi Nabiyev, and Avazagha Suleymanzade. The students were first introduced to ADA University, followed by an exploration of the basics of algorithms through mini-games on the "code.org" platform. 22 students received certificates for successfully completing the Hour. We wish them success in the future and hope that this event serves as a starting point in their careers. For details: Visit our Instagram Post
